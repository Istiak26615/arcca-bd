Member Details

Krishnendu Deb Roy

Experience:

Krishnendu Deb Roy (known as K D Roy) is a Fellow Chartered Accountant, engaged in public practice specializing in fiscal matters of Bangladesh.

K D Roy is engaged in the fiscal practice since 2002; has expertise in corporate tax assessment; first appeal & appellate tribunal; Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR); personal & expatriate taxation services; out-bound taxation services; Transfer Pricing; Tax, Financial & Secretarial Due Diligence; Forensic Services; other regulatory services and advisory services in relation to the Income Tax Ordinance (Act no. 36 of 1984) 1984, Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA), The VAT & SD Act (Act no. 47 of 2012) 2012, Customs Act (Act no. 04 of 1969) 1969, The Companies Act (Act no. 18 of 1994) 1994, Foreign Exchange Regulation Act (Act no. 07 of 1947) 1947, Bangladesh Labour Act (Act no. 42 of 2006) 2006 read with Bangladesh Labour (amended) Act (Act no. 30 of 2013) 2013, assurance services and other emerging, related laws.

K D worked directly with late Mr. A K Chowdhury, CMC, FCS, FCFC, FAFP, FCA; Past President ICAB (2000), Founder & Managing Partner of Hoda Vasi Chowdhury & Co. (Previously A F Ferguson, Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu), Chartered Accountants for 08 (eight) years. K D also worked as a partner in Pinaki & Company, Chartered Accountants for 05 years.

In 2020, K D joined Masih Muhith Haque & Co., Chartered Accountants as Partner (Taxes) and Branch-In-Charge, Dhaka Paltan Branch. K D looks after all fiscal matters of more than 100 clients of 04 offices of Masih Muhith Haque & Co including various types of clients like PLC (Public Listed Company), PIE (Public Interest Entity), banks, insurance companies, financial institutions, foreign branch & liaison offices, INGOs, garments, textiles, pharmaceutical companies, other manufacturing industries, etc. Besides, K D is also providing advisory services to local group of companies, MNC, INGO, foreign companies regarding tax & VAT matters of Bangladesh. K D is acting as a VAT Agent of foreign companies as well.

K D Roy is maintaining a team of 50 professionals in 04 offices covering tax services in Chattogram, Sylhet, Jessore, Bogura in addition to Dhaka Division.

K D is operating a website which can be treated as a hub (www.kdroy.com.bd) of tax information since 1984 including all clarifications, SROs, Finance Acts, DTAAs, etc. so that all information can be obtained digitally and swiftly. Additionally, a quarterly tax newspaper named “Bangladesh Tax Updates” is going to be published with updated tax information. K D published tax law books, TDS & VDS booklets in both printed and digital forms for sharing updated tax information to all end users.
